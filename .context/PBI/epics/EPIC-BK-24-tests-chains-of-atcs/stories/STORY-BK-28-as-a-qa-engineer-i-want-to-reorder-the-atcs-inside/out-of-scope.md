# BK-28 — Out Of Scope

> Jira field: `customfield_10135` · [View in Jira](https://upexgalaxy67.atlassian.net/browse/BK-28)

## Out of scope

- Adding a new ATC to the chain or removing one (separate concern — not covered by reorder)
- Editing the Test's title or any other metadata (backlog)
- Deleting or archiving the Test (backlog)
- Duplicating a Test as a starting point (backlog)
- Running the Test (lives in the Manual Runs epic, BK-006)
- Reorder undo / rollback to a previous chain version (Phase 2 — Test history)
- Bulk reorder across multiple Tests in one operation (out of MVP entirely)
- Reordering inside a parametrized Test variant (Phase 3 — parametrization)
- Live notification to teammates the moment a reorder is saved (covered when the Realtime epic lands)
- Diff visualization showing "before vs after" the reorder (nice-to-have, deferred until UX research)
