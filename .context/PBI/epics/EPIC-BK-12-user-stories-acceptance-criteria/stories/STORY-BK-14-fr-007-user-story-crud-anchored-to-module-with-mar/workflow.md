# BK-14 — Workflow

> Jira field: `customfield_10161` · [View in Jira](https://upexgalaxy67.atlassian.net/browse/BK-14)

1. Elena opens a Module and chooses ***New User Story***.
2. She writes a title and a Markdown description, and optionally pastes the key of a related Jira issue (for example `BK-42`).
3. On save, the Story appears in the Module's Story list with its context.
4. She can reopen it to edit the title or description; the Jira link, once set, stays fixed.
5. Removing a Story archives it — it leaves the active list but is retained.
